1 The Problem

Important emails get buried. When you're away from your desktop, a critical email can sit unread for hours. Checking email constantly to stay on top of things is disruptive — but missing an important message because you weren't watching your inbox is worse.

The solution needed to watch the inbox automatically, understand what each email says, and push a clear summary to wherever the person actually is — their phone via WhatsApp or Telegram, or a forwarded digest via Gmail.

2 How It Works

The workflow runs continuously via IMAP, triggering instantly on any new email without polling delays.

Workflow Flow

IMAP Email Trigger → Convert to Markdown → AI Summarise (GPT-4o) → Send via Gmail Send via Telegram Send via WhatsApp

The IMAP trigger monitors the inbox in real time. Each new email is converted to clean Markdown (stripping HTML clutter) before being passed to GPT-4o, which generates a concise 2-4 sentence summary capturing the sender, subject, key points, and any required action. The summary is then pushed simultaneously to Telegram, WhatsApp, and Gmail — whichever channel the recipient is active on, the message reaches them.
